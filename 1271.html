3D Darts - The Virtual Pub Experience

Darts has established itself as a popular sport with a lot of fans especially in countries like Germany, The Netherlands and the UK. But besides that, it’s one of the most popular activities on any night-out with friends and even at home. But haven’t you ever thought about playing darts when there is absolutely no chance to actually do so? Sure, you could carry a dart boards and some darts with you at all times. But from our experience people in the train start to look at you suspiciously when you try mounting a dart board to the wall and start throwing little pointy metal arrows in the cabin.

That’s where our 3D Darts game comes in very handy. Because it’s digital and on your phone (or PC), but still captures the feeling of playing real Darts. With a swipe of your finger or mouse cursor you send the darts flying and hope they hit the board where you intended. Of course that’s highly dependent on your skill. And that means you have to train your swiping finger in sensitivity and delicacy to give your dart just the right amount of speed and of course the right direction. But other than that 3D Darts works just the real thing.

Play rounds of 501, 301 or 101 against the computer in three difficulty settings. The first to reach 0 wins. Once you have mastered the game you can start to challenge a friend on the same device in 3D Darts two-player mode. And just like in a pub, there are not many things more satisfying than beating a friend with a Triple-20 when he least expects it!

Features:

  • HTML5 3D Darts
  • Single- and multiplayer modes
  • Classic 501, 301 and 101 game rounds
  • Easy swipe controls
