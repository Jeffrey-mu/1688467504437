3D Free Kick

To shoot a spectacular goal you need exactly two swipes in this football game. The first one gets the ball moving. You can control the direction and altitude of your shot with the first flick. The faster you swipe, the higher and faster the ball will fly.

After you sent the ball flying you now have control over the spin. Swipe again to change the balls direction mid-air to trick the goalkeeper. While he starts jumping in the wrong direction, sit back and watch how your shot flawlessly lands in the corner of the goal.

While you progress and score free kick after free kick, the opposing team will start upgrading their defenses by placing more and more players in front of the goal. So you will have to develop your freekicking and ball spinning skills accordingly. And it’s not only about scoring goals, but receiving the highest possible score for each goal. There are three different areas within the goal that will award you with extra points:

Crossbar:

If you place your shot directly under the crossbar of the goal, you will be rewarded with 40 points instead of just 15 for a regular goal.

Goal post:

Scoring a goal close to the goal posts on either side will get you 70 points.

Corner:

Circle your ball into the left or right upper corners and get the maximum score: 90 points

In 3D Freekick you have 3 balls to score as much points as you can. From time to time you will have the chance to receive an additional ball if you can hit the corresponding target in the goal.

Are you skilled enough to beat the goalkeeper with every shot?
